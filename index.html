Events

Prayukti 2k23

Prayukti, HIT Haldia is the annual techno-management festival of the Haldia Institute of Technology.
Prayukti originated in 2001 to provide a platform for the Indian student community to develop
and showcase their technical prowess.
It attracts people from all walks of life, including students, academia, professionals, and the general public.
So call your friends, mark your calendars, and gear up for Prayukti 2023

Riviera 2k23

The annual cultural fest of Haldia Institute of Technology, Riviera hosts a vibrant palette of event.
This 2 day event is full of music,dance and many more colourfull events.
So Be Ready for Riviera 2023.
